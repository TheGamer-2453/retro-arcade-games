I created all of these games using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. HTML was used to structure the game elements and interface, CSS was used to design and style the visuals, and JavaScript was used to implement the game logic, controls, scoring systems, and interactive features that make the games playable.

Single Player Games

Black Jack - In Blackjack, your goal is to get a card total as close to 21 as possible without going over. Number cards keep their value, face cards are worth 10, and Aces count as 11. Choose Hit to draw another card or Stay to keep your hand and compare it with the dealer's.

<img width="1874" height="1003" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/64c52805-7418-4d0a-8314-dbeb58ca708a" />

BreakOut - The objective is to destroy all the bricks by bouncing a ball off a paddle you control. Move the paddle left or right using the Arrow keys or A and D keys to keep the ball in play and hit the bricks until the wall is cleared.

<img width="1868" height="1005" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ec9cb1fd-d035-41eb-b27d-b9f18918a80a" />

Duck Hunt - The objective of Duck Hunt is to shoot the birds by tapping on them. Each bird you hit increases your score, and the more birds you successfully shoot, the higher your score becomes.

<img width="1871" height="1005" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d2200ff0-840c-438d-a90f-1a16017d4efb" />

Pac Man - The objective of Pac-Man is to clear the maze by eating all the Pac-Dots while avoiding the four colored ghosts. Move Pac-Man using the Arrow keys or WASD keys. If a ghost catches you, you lose a life. Collect all the dots to win the game.

<img width="1868" height="995" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b37c6f8f-d9de-4701-8007-dde738e63727" />

Snake - The objective of Snake is to grow the snake by eating food that appears on the screen. Move the snake using the Arrow keys or WASD keys. Each piece of food increases the snake's length and your score. Avoid hitting the walls or the snake's own body to stay alive and win the game.

<img width="1861" height="997" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/662d1fc9-683e-4463-8893-e4cd1197be6d" />

Sudoku - The objective of Sudoku is to fill every empty cell in the grid with numbers from 1 to 9. Select a number from the options below and click on the desired box to place it. Each row, column, and 3×3 box must contain every number from 1 to 9 exactly once. Complete the grid correctly to win the game.

<img width="1869" height="999" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b8ef5e72-b8bc-4377-8b22-5e7b5487e881" />

Tetris - The objective of Tetris is to clear lines by arranging falling blocks to form complete horizontal rows. Move the blocks left and right using the Arrow keys or A and D keys. Press the Up Arrow or W key to rotate the blocks, and use the Down Arrow key or S key to make them fall faster. Clear as many lines as possible without letting the blocks reach the top of the screen.

Two Player Games 

Connect 4 - The objective of Connect 4 is to be the first player to connect four of your pieces in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Click on a column to drop your piece into it. Take turns placing pieces while blocking your opponent from connecting four. Connect four pieces before your opponent to win the game.

<img width="1868" height="999" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5d9edf38-a97d-4755-923d-c02911ca6e51" />

Pong - The objective of Pong is to score points by hitting the ball past your opponent’s paddle. One player moves their paddle using the W and S keys, while the other player uses the Up and Down Arrow keys. Keep the ball in play by intercepting it with your paddle and prevent your opponent from returning it. Score more points than your opponent to win the game.

<img width="1859" height="1003" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dfacc2e2-1f4e-473e-acbc-da4f9359dc9d" />

Tic Tac Toe - The objective of Tic-Tac-Toe is to be the first player to place three of your marks in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Click on an empty square to place your mark. Take turns with your opponent and try to create a line of three while blocking them from doing the same. Get three in a row to win the game.
